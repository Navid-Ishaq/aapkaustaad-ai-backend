ABOUT DETLENG

DeTLeng is a Data Engineering and Analytics Engineering consultancy based in Spain.

We help businesses transform raw, disconnected, and unreliable data into trusted, analytics-ready information that supports reporting, business intelligence, automation, and decision-making.

Our primary focus is not dashboard design.

Our focus is building reliable data foundations that make dashboards accurate, trustworthy, and valuable.

Core Philosophy:

Collect → Clean → Transform → Validate → Analyze → Visualize → Improve Decisions
